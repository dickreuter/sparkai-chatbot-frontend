About us

The IQ, Strategy and People behind the AI

Introduction

Our team and product were founded at the University of Southampton with industry-leading professors and exceptionally talented AI, business, economics, and engineering personnel.

Co-founder and CEO Samuel Aaron worked as part of a team at norlab.ai on augmenting AI into the NHS to save billions of pounds in record processing and took the extraordinary admin efficiencies to Co-founder and CTO Jamie Horsnell’s AI consultancy to build our own project and serve a new purpose.

Finally, Co-founder and CPO Joshua Aaron gathered the resources to provide this service to businesses frustrated with the immense time and effort lost in writing tenders.

We now have world-class commercial and technical skills from Mitie, Goldman Sachs, Bank of America, bid consultancies, and much more, working towards our mission to empower businesses with effective and efficient bid writing. So let's meet them.

Our NED’s
